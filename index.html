Global Meteorite Landings Dashboard

Developed by: Khadija Jumani (SP23-BDS-023)

Interactive visualization exploring historical meteorite impacts across the globe.

Geographical Distribution

Zoom and pan the map. Hover over points for details.
Legend: Circle Size = Meteorite Mass (g) | Circle Color = Meteorite Class

Meteorite Classes (Top 10)

Click a slice to filter the map by that specific class.

Impact Timeline (1800 - 2024)

Brush (click and drag) over the timeline to filter the map by year.
Y-Axis: Number of Impacts | X-Axis: Year
